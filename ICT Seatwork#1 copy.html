War Thunder - Realistice Military Vehicles and Online Combat

One of my favorite online games to play is War Thunder. War Thunder is a free-to-play combat multiplayer that was developed and published by Gaijin Entertainment. War Thunder is a game were you can take part in major battles which are on land, air, and sea, fighting with millions of players from all over the world in an ever-evolving environment. In War Thunder you can play as different countries which have their own unique vehciles that have different capabilites in combat.

Why I love playing War Thunder

  • It is fun and has realistic graphics
  • There are lots of military veichles you can use.
  • You can take part in different battles in land, air, and at sea.
